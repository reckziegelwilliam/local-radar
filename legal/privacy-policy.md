# Privacy Policy for Buzzy

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Introduction

Buzzy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Buzzy (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information**
- Email address (for authentication)
- Anonymized user handle (automatically generated, e.g., "user_12345678")

**Event Information**
- Event titles and descriptions you create
- Event categories you select
- Event start and end times
- Photos you upload for events (optional)

**User Interactions**
- Event RSVPs
- Event reports you submit

### Information Automatically Collected

**Location Information**
- Your device's precise location when you use the app (only when app is open)
- Location is used to:
  - Display nearby events within a 5km radius
  - Create events at your current location
- **Important**: We do NOT store your location history. Location is used only for real-time proximity calculations and is not permanently saved to your profile.

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

**Usage Information**
- App features you access
- Events you view and interact with
- Time spent in the app
- Error logs and crash reports

**Push Notification Token**
- Device push notification token (to send you notifications about nearby events)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Display events near your location
   - Allow you to create and manage events
   - Enable RSVP functionality
   - Send notifications about nearby events (if you opt in)

2. **Maintain Security and Safety**
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Detect and prevent spam, fraud, and abuse
   - Respond to user reports of inappropriate content
   - Automatically hide events with multiple reports

3. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Optimize app performance

4. **Communicate with You**
   - Send push notifications about nearby events (optional)
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Send important service announcements

## How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

**Public Information**
- Event titles, categories, photos, and times you create are visible to all users near the event location
- Your anonymized user handle is visible to other users
- RSVP counts are visible to all users

**Service Providers**
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services (located in the United States)
- **Expo**: App development and deployment platform
- **Cloud Storage**: Photo storage services

**Legal Requirements**
- We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
  - Comply with legal processes
  - Enforce our Terms of Service
  - Protect our rights, property, or safety
  - Protect the rights, property, or safety of others

## Data Retention

- **Events**: Automatically deleted 6 hours after the event end time
- **Photos**: Deleted when associated event is deleted
- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **RSVPs and Reports**: Deleted when associated events are deleted

## Your Rights and Choices

**Location Permissions**
- You can disable location access at any time in your device settings
- Note: The app requires location access to function properly

**Push Notifications**
- You can disable push notifications in your device settings

**Account Deletion**
- Contact us at [support email] to request account deletion
- All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Data Access**
- Contact us to request a copy of your data

## Children's Privacy

Buzzy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted database connections
- Row-level security policies
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International Users
